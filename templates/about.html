About MockMate AI

MockMate AI is an AI-powered mock interview platform designed to help students and job seekers prepare for technical interviews. It simulates realistic interview experiences using Google's Gemini AI.

Features

  • 🤖 AI-generated interview questions
  • 🏢 Company-specific interview preparation
  • 📊 AI-generated performance report
  • 🎯 Multiple difficulty levels
  • ⏱️ Custom interview duration

Technologies Used

Python
Flask
Gemini API
HTML5
CSS3
JavaScript

Developer

Developed by Moksh Thakur as a B.Tech CSE (AI & ML) project to help students practice interviews in an interactive and intelligent way.
